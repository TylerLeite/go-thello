Game Logic
================================================================================
single player
	Game object
		board
		requirements to win
		player 1 (ai)
		player 2 (human)

	prompt_move
		get input / call ai for move
		place_piece()

	place_piece
		check if move is legal
		puts piece on board
		checks captures
		if (is_move_over)
			in_between()
			check_wincons()
			change the turn to the other guy
			prompt_move()
		else
			prompt_move()

	do_captures
		make captures happen
		check if supa kyapucha
		if (chain reactions & supa kyapucha)
			do chain reactions

	is_move_over
		lets certain character move twice
			rock with face 2
			olga 2

	in_between
		-edit board
			steal pieces
			move pieces
			remove pieces
			make pieces invisible

	check_wincons
		-number of supa kyapuchas
		-percent of board controlled
		-did you winned
		-number special tiles (critters)
		-compare to requirements
